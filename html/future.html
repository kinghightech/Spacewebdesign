Scroll down to learn about the future!

1. Currently happening:

Sun Brightens

The Sun slowly gets hotter and brighter.

2. In 30–50 million years:

Mars Gets Rings

Phobos may break apart and form rings around Mars.

3. In 600 million years:

Plants Die Out

Earth loses too much CO₂ for many plants to survive.

4. In 1 billion years:

Oceans Evaporate

The Sun gets bright enough to start cooking Earth’s oceans.

5. In 1–1.5 billion years:

Earth Becomes Unlivable

The planet becomes too hot for normal life.

6. In 2–3 billion years:

Magnetic Field Weakens

Earth may lose protection from solar radiation.

7. In 4 billion years:

Earth Turns Venus-like

The surface becomes extremely hot and hostile.

8. In 5–6 billion years:

8. Sun Runs Out of Fuel

The Sun starts leaving its normal stable stage.

9. In 6–7.5 billion years:

Sun Becomes Red Giant

The Sun expands massively and destroys inner planets.

10. After that:

White Dwarf

The leftover core becomes a small dead star.
